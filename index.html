What is SEO ?
It is the process of optimising your website so that it attracts organic or unpaid traffic from major search engines such as Google, Bing, and DuckDuckGo's results pages. The stronger your SEO, the more likely your site will appear at the top of search results and attract more visitors.
Why is SEO so Important ?
Businesses can use SEO to connect with users who are actively looking for them. Users that arrive at your website via Google search results are the most valued visitors because they are most likely to buy from you. Organic SEO promotes confidence and credibility in your business by assisting in the increase of relevant traffic.
